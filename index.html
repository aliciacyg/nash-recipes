Drinks

  • 1/4 cup lemon juice, sugar, water (each)
  • 2 more cups water
  • 6 ice cubes
  1. Heat up water, sugar, lemon juice until sugar dissolves
  2. Add the ice
  3. Let sit in fridge for 2+ hours
